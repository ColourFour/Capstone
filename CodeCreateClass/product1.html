StudyBuddy Pro

Example for the Mini Product Site + Interactive Demo project. Your actual project should have similar sections, but you can change the theme.

Hero Section

Turn your homework mountain into a tiny hill.

Planner Focus Timer Rewards
  • Big title: “StudyBuddy Pro”.
  • One-sentence description: what does your app do?
  • One main button: “Start Planning” or “Get Started”.

Features Section

Show off at least three killer features.

  • Smart Schedule – automatically spreads your tasks across the week.
  • Focus Mode – 25-minute timer with short breaks built in.
  • XP & Levels – gain XP every time you complete a task.

Interactive Demo

A tiny quiz or mini-game that matches the app.

  • Example: 4-question quiz: “What kind of learner are you?”
  • Buttons for each answer (A/B/C). Use JavaScript to count which type is most chosen.
  • At the end, show a message: “You are a Visual Learner” with a short tip.
